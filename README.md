## Led lamp 5m stripe (remote controll+colorselector)
* active: 6-8 W
* standby: 1 W

## Mid-end gaming PC
* watching youtube: 90 W
* playing games: 140 W
* hibernate: 1 W


## Hi-end gaming PC
* playing games: 300 W
* hibernate: 1 W

## 40" LED TV
* on: 130 W
* off: 1 W
 
## phone/tablet charger (microusb, 5V 0.7A)

* standby connected 
* standby not-connected
